About

What do we do? - We meet weekly to practice our English in a safe, free and friendly café.

What goes on? - We chat, we play games, study and learn together to improve our English and make new friends.

Support us

Volunteer
Anyone can volunteer with us

Help others learn, help with refreshments, help tidy up or just help by chatting. Any time you can contribute helps.

Donate
No time to spare? Please make a donation via The Good Exchange

The cafe is free to users so we need funds to cover food & drink, volunteer travel and other costs.

Food & drink
Do you enjoy baking and sharing?
